Knowledge Index Tyre System

Multi-Stint Analyser

Upload a screenshot per stint to get cross-compound wear data, compound comparison, and Discord-ready reports.

Taking screenshots

Two methods work, use whichever suits you. Both require the tyre wear bars to be visible in the HUD at the bottom of the screen.

  1. Flat screen HUD (recommended): While driving, take a PS5 screenshot as you cross the start/finish line. The lap counter on screen is your laps completed for that stint.
  2. Replay method: After your race, open the GT7 replay. For each stint except the last, scrub to the moment your car enters the pit lane. For the final stint, scrub to the finish line crossing.

Filling in the form

Pick your track and the weather you ran. Enter total race laps and the wear multiplier. For each stint, choose the compound, enter the lap number you pitted on (or your final lap for the last stint), and upload the screenshot.

Reading the results

Each stint card shows wear remaining per tyre, percentage worn per lap, and the limiting tyre. The compound comparison row at the bottom shows your average wear rate side by side across compounds, useful for picking strategy next race.

Saving sessions

Copy the session code at the bottom and paste it back later to reload your full analysis without uploading screenshots again.

Tip: KITS is mobile-first. Tap and hold images to save them to your phone if you want a Discord-friendly capture.
